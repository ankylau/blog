从美国的Nelly、A$AP Rocky到亚洲的Keith Ape、Dok2等等，饶舌歌手们一般都有这三宝：金链、撒钱、牙口好。

咦，牙口好是什么情况？看完上面Nelly的动态图还不懂吗，那就是越来越多人开始拥有的膨胀级饰品——

金牙（Grillz）

<img class="alignnone wp-image-918 size-full" src="https://anky.oss-cn-qingdao.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/8bf41f1abcbb075befa3693a60b088ab.gif" width="540" height="300" /> 

● 一般你最早接触的是小时候家里年纪大一点的长辈镶在嘴里的那一两颗。

● 后来开始听欧美音乐的你会发现，某些摇滚歌手玩个性，大部分嘻哈艺人炫富耍帅用的就是它。

● 现在亚洲的饶舌歌手们也不落下，而且越来越多潮人戴起金牙。少女MT个人认为，把金牙带到潮流人视线中的是“Jiggy Nigga” A$AP Rocky。

<img class="alignnone wp-image-919 size-full" src="https://anky.oss-cn-qingdao.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/e30fea89cdaba8f775835fa83876b3c0.jpeg" width="500" height="500" srcset="https://anky.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/e30fea89cdaba8f775835fa83876b3c0.jpeg 500w, https://anky.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/e30fea89cdaba8f775835fa83876b3c0-300x300.jpeg 300w, https://anky.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/e30fea89cdaba8f775835fa83876b3c0-100x100.jpeg 100w, https://anky.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/e30fea89cdaba8f775835fa83876b3c0-150x150.jpeg 150w, https://anky.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/e30fea89cdaba8f775835fa83876b3c0-200x200.jpeg 200w" sizes="(max-width: 500px) 100vw, 500px" /> 

A$AP Rocky敲经典的时装周街拍，with 满嘴金牙。

● 和过去长辈们镶的不同，今天要说的金牙（Grillz）是一种可拆卸重复佩戴，镶着金子和钻石的装饰性牙套。如果你箍过牙，佩戴的原理可以参考拆掉牙套后戴的保持器。

现在科普给你

关于Grillz的四件事

金牙是真的金子做的？

<img class="alignnone wp-image-920 size-full" src="https://anky.oss-cn-qingdao.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/e8f0d5499cd69053517eb54cd8356fc7.jpeg" width="550" height="367" srcset="https://anky.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/e8f0d5499cd69053517eb54cd8356fc7.jpeg 550w, https://anky.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/e8f0d5499cd69053517eb54cd8356fc7-300x200.jpeg 300w, https://anky.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/e8f0d5499cd69053517eb54cd8356fc7-150x100.jpeg 150w" sizes="(max-width: 550px) 100vw, 550px" /> 

既然俗称“大金牙”了，Grillz的材质自然也是金子，并且和常见的首饰一样，分18K金和24K金，做好后，再在上面镶上钻石等Bling Bling的装饰。

<img class="alignnone wp-image-921 size-full" src="https://anky.oss-cn-qingdao.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/9fc2c6873b50026ecbccd548563fc959.jpeg" width="500" height="498" srcset="https://anky.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/9fc2c6873b50026ecbccd548563fc959.jpeg 500w, https://anky.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/9fc2c6873b50026ecbccd548563fc959-300x300.jpeg 300w, https://anky.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/9fc2c6873b50026ecbccd548563fc959-100x100.jpeg 100w, https://anky.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/9fc2c6873b50026ecbccd548563fc959-150x150.jpeg 150w, https://anky.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/9fc2c6873b50026ecbccd548563fc959-200x200.jpeg 200w" sizes="(max-width: 500px) 100vw, 500px" /> 

MT少女在这里为大家小小科普一下，一般将金子纯度划分为24份，24K金即是足金，而作为首饰来说舒适度最好的18K金，就是纯度为18/24，混入了其他贵金属的合金。

<img class="alignnone wp-image-922 size-full" src="https://anky.oss-cn-qingdao.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/9e15fa63fd8e52d1f3f6a71fb2caa793.jpeg" width="610" height="728" srcset="https://anky.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/9e15fa63fd8e52d1f3f6a71fb2caa793.jpeg 610w, https://anky.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/9e15fa63fd8e52d1f3f6a71fb2caa793-300x358.jpeg 300w, https://anky.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/9e15fa63fd8e52d1f3f6a71fb2caa793-600x716.jpeg 600w, https://anky.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/9e15fa63fd8e52d1f3f6a71fb2caa793-126x150.jpeg 126w, https://anky.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/9e15fa63fd8e52d1f3f6a71fb2caa793-251x300.jpeg 251w" sizes="(max-width: 610px) 100vw, 610px" /> 

所以呢，我们常见的Grillz是18k金的~

Grillz是怎么来的？

<img class="alignnone wp-image-923 size-full" src="https://anky.oss-cn-qingdao.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/5ef18222b60d49c0b1b26c86360c11c5.jpeg" width="590" height="550" srcset="https://anky.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/5ef18222b60d49c0b1b26c86360c11c5.jpeg 590w, https://anky.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/5ef18222b60d49c0b1b26c86360c11c5-300x280.jpeg 300w, https://anky.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/5ef18222b60d49c0b1b26c86360c11c5-150x140.jpeg 150w" sizes="(max-width: 590px) 100vw, 590px" /> 

除了常规的金镶钻，Grillz从形状到钻石图案，品类花式越来越多，而最早的“金牙”，则是公元前有钱人的专属饰品（虽然现在也是��）。

＃元祖“膨胀饰物”＃

为牙齿镶上金属，源于古埃及和玛雅人的古文明。公元前2000年时，古埃及人就开始在牙齿上钻孔，镶入金子、珠宝，作为财富的象征。这种金牙是真·始祖级潮人最初代的膨胀饰品。

<img class="alignnone wp-image-925 size-full" src="https://anky.oss-cn-qingdao.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/f6a4e8e04fd73c0aabb3ef6c2747ed07.jpeg" width="960" height="325" srcset="https://anky.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/f6a4e8e04fd73c0aabb3ef6c2747ed07.jpeg 960w, https://anky.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/f6a4e8e04fd73c0aabb3ef6c2747ed07-300x102.jpeg 300w, https://anky.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/f6a4e8e04fd73c0aabb3ef6c2747ed07-600x203.jpeg 600w, https://anky.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/f6a4e8e04fd73c0aabb3ef6c2747ed07-150x51.jpeg 150w, https://anky.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/f6a4e8e04fd73c0aabb3ef6c2747ed07-768x260.jpeg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 960px) 100vw, 960px" /> 

之后在中世纪的欧洲以及中亚地区，先后由Giovanni d’Arcoli和Robert Arthur两位牙医推动发展，出现了和现在的Grillz相似的使用了K金的金牙。

<img class="alignnone wp-image-924 size-full" src="https://anky.oss-cn-qingdao.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/3d2e1e006013242450f7eb512e20f111.jpeg" width="700" height="300" srcset="https://anky.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/3d2e1e006013242450f7eb512e20f111.jpeg 700w, https://anky.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/3d2e1e006013242450f7eb512e20f111-300x129.jpeg 300w, https://anky.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/3d2e1e006013242450f7eb512e20f111-600x257.jpeg 600w, https://anky.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/3d2e1e006013242450f7eb512e20f111-150x64.jpeg 150w" sizes="(max-width: 700px) 100vw, 700px" /> 

随着时代继续发展，欧洲人在美洲开发起了殖民地，地主、农场主们会为奴隶们镶上有着自家专属图案的金牙，来分辨奴隶们的身份、等级（这一段也是hiphop歌手们不太愿意提起的文化历史）。

<img class="alignnone wp-image-928 size-full" src="https://anky.oss-cn-qingdao.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/bda0547e368117ef51ab1ff18e6acf8a.jpeg" width="700" height="366" srcset="https://anky.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/bda0547e368117ef51ab1ff18e6acf8a.jpeg 700w, https://anky.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/bda0547e368117ef51ab1ff18e6acf8a-300x157.jpeg 300w, https://anky.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/bda0547e368117ef51ab1ff18e6acf8a-600x314.jpeg 600w, https://anky.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/bda0547e368117ef51ab1ff18e6acf8a-150x78.jpeg 150w" sizes="(max-width: 700px) 100vw, 700px" /> 

全面带起Grillz风潮的白人Raper Paul Wall

现在无论欧美还是亚洲，嘻哈艺人和时尚icon们都在戴的Grillz，则是HipHop Bling Bling文化的产物。1980－90年代，金牙教父（Godfather of Grillz）Eddie Plein最早在纽约做起了Grillz，并推动当时纽约一种老牌RapperFlavor Flav, Big Daddy Kane和Kool G Rap带起了Grillz。

<img class="alignnone wp-image-929 size-full" src="https://anky.oss-cn-qingdao.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/fffdfac013d7f16445bbb12d14127acf.jpeg" width="598" height="596" srcset="https://anky.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/fffdfac013d7f16445bbb12d14127acf.jpeg 598w, https://anky.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/fffdfac013d7f16445bbb12d14127acf-300x300.jpeg 300w, https://anky.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/fffdfac013d7f16445bbb12d14127acf-100x100.jpeg 100w, https://anky.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/fffdfac013d7f16445bbb12d14127acf-150x150.jpeg 150w, https://anky.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/fffdfac013d7f16445bbb12d14127acf-200x200.jpeg 200w" sizes="(max-width: 598px) 100vw, 598px" /> 

Eddie Plein（左）和Cee Lo Green

之后“教父”Eddie Plein在亚特兰大开起了主做Grillz的首饰店，并一直经营至今，Outkast, Lil Jon, Ying Yang Twins, Lloyd, Ludacris, Nas都是他的客户。

<img class="alignnone wp-image-927 size-full" src="https://anky.oss-cn-qingdao.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/5364f8b8b468180fc851d4d020cf11f7.jpeg" width="594" height="592" srcset="https://anky.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/5364f8b8b468180fc851d4d020cf11f7.jpeg 594w, https://anky.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/5364f8b8b468180fc851d4d020cf11f7-300x300.jpeg 300w, https://anky.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/5364f8b8b468180fc851d4d020cf11f7-100x100.jpeg 100w, https://anky.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/5364f8b8b468180fc851d4d020cf11f7-150x150.jpeg 150w, https://anky.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/5364f8b8b468180fc851d4d020cf11f7-200x200.jpeg 200w" sizes="(max-width: 594px) 100vw, 594px" /> 

Eddie Plein（中）也算是HipHop界老匠人了吧？

现在能查到的资料里大多会说，南部是Grillz文化起源、发展最盛的地方。这除了跟Eddie Plein把自己的Grillz生意开在亚特兰大有关之外，更多地要归功于HipHop里Grillz文化最大的推手——白人Rapper Paul Wall。

<img class="alignnone wp-image-926 size-full" src="https://anky.oss-cn-qingdao.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/42579cd2dbd94337f8b3338d1c043ca7.jpeg" width="581" height="356" srcset="https://anky.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/42579cd2dbd94337f8b3338d1c043ca7.jpeg 581w, https://anky.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/42579cd2dbd94337f8b3338d1c043ca7-300x184.jpeg 300w, https://anky.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/42579cd2dbd94337f8b3338d1c043ca7-150x92.jpeg 150w" sizes="(max-width: 581px) 100vw, 581px" /> 

上图里这个小胖就是来自圣路易斯的老牌Rapper Paul Wall，1998年他和朋友Johny Dang开始在电视上贩卖金牙，价格大概是一颗50美金，一个牙套则要近5万。从休斯顿开始，Grillz风潮蔓延到整个南部HipHop圈。2005年时圣路易斯HipHop歌手Nelly，有Paul Wall参与的歌《Grillz》把这种文化带到了主流眼中。
